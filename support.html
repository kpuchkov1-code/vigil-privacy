Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect Apple Health?

Vigil will ask for HealthKit permissions when you first open the app. If you declined, go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > Vigil and enable the data you want to share.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open Vigil, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases". Your Vigil Pro subscription is linked to your Apple ID and will restore automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings > your name > Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel.

Is my data backed up?

All data is stored locally on your device. You can enable cloud backup in Settings to sync your data to your private iCloud account.

How does the AI coach work?

Vigil's AI mentor uses your workout history, nutrition, sleep, heart rate, habits, and goals to give you personalized advice. Your conversations are not stored on our servers.

The app is not showing my health data.

Make sure HealthKit permissions are enabled for Vigil in your iPhone's Health settings. Data may take a few moments to sync after granting access.

App Version

Make sure you are running the latest version of Vigil from the App Store for the best experience and latest bug fixes.
